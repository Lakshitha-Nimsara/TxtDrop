TextDrop

TextDrop is a free, simple, and fast online text editor built to solve a real problem: moving text between devices without needing accounts, sync apps, or cables.

🎯 Our Mission

To give everyone a free, instant, no-login tool for writing and saving plain text. Simple, clean, and useful. No unnecessary features, no paywalls.

💡 Why TextDrop?

Many people need to move text from their phone to their PC without syncing apps. TextDrop lets you type, save as .txt, and share that file via WhatsApp, email, USB, or cloud storage.

🔒 Your Privacy

TextDrop does not collect any personal data. Everything you type stays entirely in your browser. We do not log, store, or transmit your text to any server.

🛠️ Built for Everyone

TextDrop is an independently developed web tool, designed to be genuinely useful for anyone who needs a fast, no-fuss way to write and save plain text, anywhere in the world.
